How to Use
1

Thread onto your hose bib

Screw the gauge clockwise onto any outdoor spigot with ¾" garden hose threads. Hand-tight only — no wrench needed.

2

Open the valve fully

With all other water use stopped inside the house, open the hose bib completely. The glycerin-filled needle will settle within 1–2 seconds.

3

Record your reading

Read the PSI on the dial — this is your static pressure. For dynamic pressure, activate an irrigation zone and take a second reading. Enter both numbers into the Drip Atlas design tool.
